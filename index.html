Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Introduction

Solfeggio - Sight Reading ("the App") is developed by Onur Unal. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All user preferences (such as tempo and display settings) are stored locally on your device only.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Internet Access

The App may require internet access solely for verifying in-app purchases through Google Play. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
